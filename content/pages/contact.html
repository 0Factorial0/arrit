Mark Deckrent is the CEO and founder of Arrit, a furniture company that specializes in creating high-quality and stylish pieces for modern living spaces. With over 25 years of experience in the furniture industry, Mark is a passionate and skilled leader who has built Arrit into a well-respected brand.

Mark's interest in furniture design began at a young age, as he was always fascinated by the way furniture can transform a space and enhance the way people live. After completing his education in design and business, Mark started his career in the furniture industry and quickly rose through the ranks thanks to his talent and dedication.

As the CEO of Arrit, Mark is responsible for overseeing all aspects of the business, from product design and development to sales and marketing. He is known for his hands-on approach and his attention to detail, which has helped him build a loyal customer base that appreciates the quality and craftsmanship of Arrit's products.

Mark is also committed to making a positive impact on the environment and society. He ensures that Arrit's manufacturing processes are sustainable and eco-friendly, and he supports various charitable causes that help improve the lives of people in need.

Welcome to Arrit Mark Deckrent's Furniture Company, where we bring style and comfort to your living space.

Our company was founded by Arrit Mark Deckrent, who has been in the furniture industry for over 25 years. With his expertise and passion for design, he established our company with the goal of creating high-quality and affordable furniture pieces for everyone.

At Arrit Mark Deckrent's Furniture Company, we take pride in our commitment to using only the finest materials in all of our products. From our sturdy hardwood frames to our plush upholstery, we ensure that every piece of furniture we create is built to last.

Our collection includes a wide variety of sofas, chairs, tables, and other furnishings that can be customized to suit your personal taste and style. Whether you prefer classic designs or contemporary pieces, our team of experienced craftsmen can create the perfect furniture for you.

We also offer a range of services to help you get the most out of your furniture. Our in-house design team can help you select the right pieces to create the perfect look for your home, while our delivery and installation team ensures that your furniture is set up and ready to use.
